Welcome to YouPivot & TimeMarks

Traditionally, you could only find websites and files if you knew the magical key words. If you didn't, then too bad. We didn't like that, thus YouPivot and TimeMarks was born. YouPivot and TimeMarks function based on how Cognitive Psychologists think human memory works, by contextual cues. Think about what happens when you loose your car keys, people say "re-trace your steps!" With YouPivot and TimeMarks you can do that, but with your digital files. With YouPivot, now you can find that website you were reading when you were "last listening to AC/DC's Hells Bells." TimeMarks are an alternative to bookmarking one page or another – forgetting to write down a URL – or having to manage hundreds of cumbersome links. Whenever you think to yourself, "Wow, this moment is important," make a TimeMark.

YouPivot and TimeMarks - No hassle, no fuss, no organizing needed. With the click of a button, you can label, save, search and find.

Key Features:

TimeMarks

If you are doing research, programming, or even planning a trip, you may have many many tabs open simultaneously. Use a TimeMark to save everything you are doing, right now. No need to organize, or bookmark. Just click the TimeMark icon , and everything is saved. (click on the TimeMarks options tab for more details)

More Powerful History

YouPivot and TimeMarks also provides a new interface to your traditional web history. Right now, it is just cleaner and easier to use. As we release updates, we will allow you to filter your content, and perform live search for faster history traversal! (click on the traditional history options tab for more details)

YouPivot

YouPivot is an optional feature of the YouPivot and TimeMarks extension. Should you enable it, you can begin to search and browse your history (digital and real world) by context. Search for a file, based on the song you were listening to. Find that website you were reading, right before that call from mom. All With YouPivot. (click on the YouPivot options tab for more details)

TimeMarks By Location

Want to see the TimeMarks you made last time you were at work? Or maybe the TimeMarks you left at home, or in the coffee shop down the street. With our GeoLocation system, you can now see (and in the future filter) based on where you were when you TimeMarked. (click on the Geo Location options tab for more details)
